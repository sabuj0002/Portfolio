Three movies that I will suggest everyone to watch:

  • Life is beautiful

    When an open-minded Jewish waiter and his son become victims of the Holocaust, he uses a perfect mixture of will, humor and imagination to protect his son from the dangers around their camp.

  • Life of Pi

    A young man who survives a disaster at sea is hurtled into an epic journey of adventure and discovery. While cast away, he forms an unexpected connection with another survivor: a fearsome Bengal tiger.

  • Aynabaji

    The story is about Ayna, a struggling actor who gets to live his profession in real life after failing in his career. Gifted with the natural talent of acting, he is a man with a versatile character and can morph into anybody he wants.
